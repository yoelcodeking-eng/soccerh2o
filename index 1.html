Hydration Playbook

Hydration isn’t just “drink more water.” It’s timing, quantity, and balance. Here are core principles every SoccerH2O player learns.

Before Training

Start the Session Full

  • Drink steadily in the 2 hours before training, not all at once.
  • Check your urine color—aim for pale yellow, not clear or dark.
  • Add a pinch of electrolytes if it’s hot or you sweat heavily.
During Training

Small, Frequent Sips

  • Use every water break, even if you “don’t feel thirsty.”
  • Take small sips so your stomach stays comfortable while running.
  • In long sessions, mix water with an electrolyte drink.
After Training

Refuel and Rebuild

  • Drink water within 30 minutes of finishing.
  • Pair fluids with a snack that has carbs and protein.
  • Keep sipping through the evening, especially after night sessions.
Bonus

Hydration Tracker Idea

Give each player a bottle with level markers (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%) and set “checkpoints” during training. Turn it into a team challenge: if everyone hits 75% by the final whistle, the whole squad wins.
